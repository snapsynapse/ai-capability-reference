# Claude Extended Thinking — February 2026

**Query:** What is the current state of Claude Extended Thinking in February 2026?
**Model:** sonar-pro
**Date:** 2026-02-17

---

## Answer

Extended Thinking remains available as a core capability in Claude Opus 4.6 and Sonnet 4.6, enhanced by adaptive thinking and effort levels. Opus 4.6 released Feb 5, Sonnet 4.6 on Feb 17.

- **Pro plan**: Yes, accessible on $20/mo Pro via claude.ai (select Opus 4.6 + Extended Thinking or use Sonnet 4.6)
- **Adaptive thinking**: Has NOT fully replaced manual; both coexist. Adaptive is recommended (`thinking: {type: "adaptive"}`). Manual (`thinking: {type: "enabled"}`) is deprecated on Opus 4.6 but still functional temporarily.
- **Effort levels**: Yes, four levels (low, medium, high/default, max) added with Opus 4.6
