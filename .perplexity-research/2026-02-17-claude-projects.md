## Answer

Free tier users **cannot** create Claude Projects—paid-only (Pro and above).

Plans with access:
- Pro ($20/mo): Full access
- Max 5x ($100/mo): Full access
- Max 20x ($200/mo): Full access
- Team and Enterprise: Included

No 5-project limit for free — Projects simply not available to free tier at all.
